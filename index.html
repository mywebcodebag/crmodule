Introduction

Preface

All Government Bodies adhere to standard operating procedures as they orchestrate their duties regularly,

As a standard process, the Government Bodies are obligated to submit Confidential Reports (CR).

The annual Confidential Reports of the Government employees are derived with an intent to gauge performance in the areas of their work, conduct, character, and capabilities.

Objective

Confidential Report - The Objective

Assess the potential of the Government Officials (staff) and prepare them for jobs that match their skills and personality.

Assessment and feedback mechanism aids in elevating their performance in the current job and also helps build opportunities to help them scale up their career.

Confidential Reports have an important role in accurately gauging the performance of government officials as it maps their professional and interpersonal traits towards their duties and responsibilities:

Efficiency of service / work

Conduct and behavioral attributes

Individual character

Accurately assess individual caliber based on the recorded opinion

2

Super Admin Module

Super Admin Module Features

Manage Entities
 

Super Admin has the option to create and update all settings with respect to a particular entity. E.g. Edit, Deactivate, Process Flow for Super Admin General Settings, Email, SMS, etc.

Manage Offices
& Departments

Super Admin can manage the Offices and Departments of all the entities.

Dashboard
 

Holistic Dashboard as well as entity-wise Dashboard is available under the Super Admin login. Reports can be extracted in multiple file formats.

Establishment Branch Module

Establishment Branch Module Features

Manage Users

On the basis of their Grades, respective Confidential Report is made available to employees. Once submitted, the same gets listed and can be tracked.

Verify CR Section

Provision is made for the Establishment Branch to verify Confidential Reports once the same is verified by the Reporting and the Reviewing Officer.

Manage Masters

Establishment Branch has the right to manage multiple masters such as the Offices, Departments, Designations, Email Templates, and SMS Templates.

Manage General
Settings

Establishment Branch can tweak the general settings such as the logo, background images, SMS settings, email settings, colour codes, etc.

Office & Department
Wise Reports

Establishment Branch being the admin for a particular entity can extract reports: Office wise and Department wise.

Employee Statistics
& Progress

Establishment Branch can track the status of Confidential Reports of each user.
